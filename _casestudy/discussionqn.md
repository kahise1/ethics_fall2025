1. **What benefits does AI offer in skin cancer detection?**  
   Consider how AI can improve diagnostic speed, accuracy, and accessibility for patients.  

2. **How does biased training data affect diagnostic outcomes?**  
   Explore how underrepresentation of darker skin tones in datasets can lead to misdiagnosis or delayed treatment.  

3. **Is unequal accuracy an acceptable tradeoff for faster innovation?**  
   Think about the ethical implications of implementing AI that benefits some patients more than others.  

4. **Who should be held accountable when biased AI causes harm?**  
   Discuss the responsibilities of developers, healthcare providers, hospitals, and regulators.  

5. **Should AI fairness be legally required in healthcare? Why or why not?**  
   Consider how policy, law, and ethical standards might enforce equitable AI use and protect patients.
